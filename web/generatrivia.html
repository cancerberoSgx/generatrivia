generatrivia : a game board generator application

With generatrivia you can easily generate board games in your computer, and plublish them on internet. It also contains a lot of game board ready to be played, like chess, checkers, tic, tac, toe, and specially a lot of tribias.

it is designed to easily define your own games,...

Board games generated do not contain anything like a artificial intelligence: they only mean to replace the game materials, like the board, pieces, dices, etc.
